Profiles

A showcase of some of the best wrestler's today in AEW, NJPW, and WWE.

Penta El Zero Miedo

AEW

Fearless luchador known for his unique style and intense rivalries. Multiple-time tag team champion and fan-favorite.

Signature Moves: Fear Factor, Pentagon Driver, The Sacrifice

Samoa Joe

AEW

Submission specialist with a hard-hitting and intense in-ring presence. Recently became the longest Ring of Honor Television Champion.

Signature Moves: Coquina Clutch, Muscle Buster

Kenny Omega

AEW

The Best Bout Machine. Known for his high-intensity and high-impact style. Once held three belts from three different promotions, earning the nickname "The Belt Collector."

Signature Moves: One-Winged Angel, V-Trigger

Roman Reigns

WWE

"The Tribal Chief" and current WWE Champion. Known for his powerhouse style and dominant presence in WWE. He is part of the legendary Anoai' Family, which Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson is also a part of.

Signature Moves: Superman Punch, Spear

Gunther

WWE

The Ring General, renowned for his hard-hitting style and impressive stature. The current WWE Intercontinental Champion and became the longest reigning champion in his first reign.

Signature Moves: Lariat, Powerbomb, Knife-Edge Chops

Cody Rhodes

WWE

The son of "The American Dream" Dusty Rhodes, "The American Nightmare" Cody Rhodes, known for his versatility and resiliency, is one of the top stars of WWE right now.

Signature Moves: Cross Rhodes, Cody Cutter

Kazuchika Okada

NJPW

The Rainmaker, Okada was the longest reigning IWGP Heavyweight champion with a total of five reigns. His in-ring presence and ability to adapt make him a cornerstone of NJPW.

Signature Moves: Rainmaker Clothesline

Minoru Suzuki

NJPW

"The King of Pro Wrestling" and Murder Grandpa, Suzuki is a seasoned veteran known for his hard-hitting and no-nonsense style. A former NEVER Openweight Champion, he instills fear in his opponents with his submission expertise and aggressive demeanor.

Signature Moves: Gotch-Style Piledriver

Will Ospreay

NJPW

The Aerial Assassin, Ospreay is a high-flying and innovative competitor. The current IWGP "United Kingdom" Champion, he combines acrobatics with striking prowess, making him a dynamic force in NJPW.

Signature Moves: Stormbreaker, Oscutter
