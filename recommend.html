Other Recommendations

Here are some curated content that from different sources all over the web. The selection ranges from wrestling blogs to youtube channels and other websites that can help you deepen your understanding and appreciation of professional wrestling

Wrestling Blogs:

A list of blogs that contains articles and thinkpieces that brings the latest updates of wrestling, as well as making interesting discussions surronding wrestling from all over the world.

  • Voices of Wrestling

    • Voices of Wrestling have offers enthusiaistic content involving professional wrestling. They cover professional wrestling worldwide, including European wrestling, old-school wrestling, AEW, WWE, NJPW, Dragongate, lucha liibre, and more! Check out their "The Wrestling 101" series on their website to have a great overview of wrestling history, past and present.

  • Wrestling Inn

    • A home for passionate fans to write and talk about pro-wrestling. Wrestling Inn provides many content, from their featured articles to interview with various wrestlers to podcasts discussing the current landscape of the industry.

  • The Wrestling Observer

    • Perhaps the most famous (or infamous?) wrestling publication. Headed by Dave Meltzer, The Wrestling Observer is the premier newsletter regarding wrestling. Meltzer's star rating system given to the various matches in the industry have prompted many people to discuss questions like "Who is the greatest wrestler" and debate on what is a 5-star match.

YouTube Channels:

Some YouTube channels we recommend if you want to watch more about wrestling directly from documentaries, as well as seeing wrestling in action.

  • Dropmick Wrestling

    • Dropmick Wrestling produces some of the best wrestling documentaries on YouTube. Covering entire rivalries, they showcase wrestling with clips and videos in a chronological order. If you want to watch hour-long videos, without added commentary and in comprehensive sequence of events. Please check out Dropmick Wrestling's videos. Below is a clip from his coverage of Ospreay vs. Omega video.

  • Kim Justice's Wrestling Road

    • Nothing is like Puroresu (Japanese Pro-Wrestling) and Kim Justice understands this. With a focus on Puroresu, Kim Justice adds commentary and context on significant moments of various Japanese promotions Do you want to see how Vader rose from a jobber in the WWF to a fabled monster in Japan? How about seeing a video about KEnta Kobashi's legendary reign as NOAH's GHC Heavyweight Champion? Check out Kim Justice's Wrestling Road if you want to find out. Below is a snippet from his video covering the resurgence of the Tiger Driver '91.

  • Thunderfilez

    • You ever attempted a wrestling move when you were younger? OF course you have, because almost every kid ignored the disclaimer and broke furnitures attempting to do moves. Thunderfilez is a YouTube channel that showcases the moves performed by wrestlers. Some are high-flying wondrs, while others are brutal that makes you flinch in shock and awe. Below you can find a clip of Thuderfilez's collection of Kenta Kobashi's 7 Uses of the Buring Hammer, the most infamous finisher in pro wrestling history.

Other Websites:

These are other places we recommend to also visit to jump in the world of professional wrestling.

    Cagematch

    • The Internet's Wrestling Database. It archives almost everything and anything pro-wrestling related. It has lists of wrestlers, promotions, events, and matches. Check out their lists, like "The Top 100 Matches of All Time" if you want to find the finest examples of pro wrestling.

  • r/SquaredCircle

    • The subreddit r/SquaredCircle is a dedicated forum to professional wresting in Reddit. Catch up-to-date news and discussions surrounding the IWC with other enthusiasts.

  • All Elite Moves

    • A neat little website that is still a work in progress. It lists all the roster members of the promotion All Elite Wrestling (AEW). ut this website showcases the moveset of every roster member. Check it out if you want to dive deeper into the movesets of your favorite AEW wrestler.
