Regions

United States

The United States has a rich history of professional wrestling with various promotions such as WWE, AEW, and Impact Wrestling. Styles range from the traditional American style to high-flying and technical wrestling.

The United Kingdom

The UK has produced many talented wrestlers and is known for promotions like WWE's NXT UK, Progress Wrestling, and ICW. British wrestling often combines strong technical wrestling with a strong emphasis on storytelling.

Mexico

Mexican wrestling, or Lucha Libre, is famous for its high-flying acrobatics and colorful characters. Promotions like AAA and CMLL showcase the unique Mexican wrestling style, characterized by the use of masks and fast-paced action.

Japan

Japan has a diverse wrestling scene with promotions like NJPW, AJPW, and Stardom. Japanese wrestling is known for its strong style, technical prowess, and a strong focus on the competitive aspect of the sport.
